Welcome to Distill, your all-in-one voice inbox application. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our application.

1. Introduction

Distill ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

  • Device model, Android version, app version
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Performance and analytics data
  • Network status
  • Voice recordings and speech transcripts only when you choose to use voice features
  • Name or email address only if you voluntarily provide them (such as contacting support)

3. Why We Collect Data

  • Provide speech-to-text and AI features
  • Generate summaries and responses
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Fix bugs and prevent abuse
  • Provide customer support

4. Permissions

  • Internet – required for AI and speech processing.
  • Network State – checks connectivity.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted providers such as Google Firebase (Analytics/Crashlytics/Firebase/Authentication), Gemini Speech to Text and Groq for AI processing and Speech to Text. They process only the information necessary to provide requested features under their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only with service providers required to operate the app.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Audio and transcripts may be deleted after processing unless you choose to save them.

8. Security

We use HTTPS encryption, access controls, and industry-standard safeguards to protect your information.

9. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

10. Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 or the minimum legal age in your jurisdiction.

11. International Processing

Your information may be processed on servers located in countries other than your own depending on the services used.
